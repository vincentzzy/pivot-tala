About Team Pivot


Hailing from Tabaco National High School, Team Pivot is a group of 4 STEM Students who don't know stuff about coding but decided to enter a competition because being alive is expensive and cash prizes are great.

Jokes aside, Team Pivot is a group of dedicated procrastinators who have big ideas and the limited willpower to build it. We believe in the power of motivation, and we are more than motivated to change up the system we are in: education. Our name, Pivot, reflects our desire to be the people who will be the turning point in our journey to find the best (or worst) solution in our problems, whatever is easier.
