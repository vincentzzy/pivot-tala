Mathematics Fundamentals

Let’s say you have 4 apples, and you picked another 2 off a tree. Now you have 6 apples.

That process is called addition!

Now let’s say you gave your friend 3 apples from your 6 apples. Now you have only 3 apples left.

That’s called subtraction!

Let’s say you have a lot more apples, and you want to give 3 of your friends 3 apples each. How many apples did you give your friends?

9!

And what do you call that process? Multiplication!

But what if you had only 8 apples and you want to give your 4 friends the same number of apples? That’s what you call division!

In this lesson, we’ll tour you to the building blocks in math: the 4 basic operations, and how you can use it in the real world; in money, in food, and other things too!
