Addition

Addition is the process of adding 2 values together. Let’s go back to our original example. You have 4 apples, and you picked 2 more. You can visualize that as

(image of 4 apples beside 2 more apples)

How many do you have now? Simply count all that you have after adding the 2 values together. Now you have 6!
If that doesn’t fancy you, you can always use the number line! What is a number line? It’s numbers… on a line. With negative integers on the left (more on that topic soon), zero at the center, and positive integers on the left, it gives you a logical progression of numbers.
