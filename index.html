Welcome to Project TALA!

By Students. For Students.


Project TALA is a student initiative to provide basic education to interested or struggling students or people without proper access to education.

Access the first prototype here! And our changelog here!

Why Project TALA?

It's Free. Forever.

Project TALA is an open-source free education platform designed by students to help other students in areas where assistance is greatly helpful.

Anytime. Anywhere.

Project TALA is designed to be light on your data and light on your time.

Works With You!

hopefully :T
Project TALA is designed to learn your preferences with a single survey and offer lessons based off of what we think might work best with your studying style. If you find any problems with this system, contact us!
